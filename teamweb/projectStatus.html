The Phases of Agile Development


Source: http://www.hellopeople.com.au/agile-software-development-perth.html



Planning & Requirements Analysis:


Meet with client to determine which User Stories will be completed during the Sprint.

Design Phase:


Designing and planning ways to implement and complete the tasks required for the given Sprint.

Build Phase (Current Project Phase):


Implementing the designs created in the design phase.

Testing Phase:


Making sure the deliverable meets all the test criteria.

Evaluation Phase:


Meet with the client to discuss whether or not the Acceptance Criteria was met for each User Story
